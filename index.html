Why the Computer Science Academy?

The Computer Science Academy (CSA) empowers students through specialized pathways that prepare them for the technology-driven future. Our academy offers hands-on experience in cutting-edge fields including AI, Cybersecurity, Programming, and Data Analytics.

Students will have the opportunity to earn industry-recognized credentials and participate in real-world learning experiences that bridge the gap between classroom theory and professional practice.

Transform data into actionable insights

Industry Certification

Academy Eligibility

Already Enrolled

Incoming 9th Grade Students currently enrolled in either Honors Java or AP Computer Science Principles (CSP)

Eligible & Interested

Incoming 9th Grade Students not currently enrolled but meets placement criteria and requests a course change through Guidance

Not Yet Eligible

Incoming 9th Grade Students may qualify by taking a placement test or enrolling in a Python summer course to be eligible for CSP

Curricular Highlights

AP Computer Science Principles

Introduction to the foundational concepts of computer science and programming

AP Computer Science A & Cyber 1: Networking Fundamentals

Advanced programming in Java and introduction to networking concepts

Advanced Robotics

Hands-on experience with robotics design, programming, and implementation

AP Cyber 2: Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Advanced cybersecurity concepts, practices, and defensive techniques

Capstone Project

Culminating project demonstrating mastery of computer science concepts

Enhanced Electives

Specialized courses in emerging technologies and advanced topics
